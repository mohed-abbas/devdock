### GAP-5 — Terminal service DATABASE_URL (approach B1)
- **D-05:** Add a single `DATABASE_URL` line to the `terminal` service `environment:` block in `docker-compose.yml`, mirroring the app service's line 88:
  ```yaml
  DATABASE_URL: postgresql://${POSTGRES_USER:-devdock}:${POSTGRES_PASSWORD}@postgres:5432/${POSTGRES_DB:-devdock}
  ```
